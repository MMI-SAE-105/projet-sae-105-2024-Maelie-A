Glossary

"Find definitions for key terms related to sustainable fashion."

Written on October 20, 2024, at 5:00 PM

Organic Cotton

Cotton grown without chemical pesticides or synthetic fertilizers, adhering to sustainable farming practices that preserve soil and reduce environmental impact.

Denim

A sturdy fabric commonly used for jeans. Sustainable production includes the use of organic or recycled cotton and non-polluting dyeing processes.

Carbon Footprint

The amount of greenhouse gases emitted during a product's lifecycle, from raw material production to final distribution.

Fast Fashion

A textile production model focused on speed and constant collection renewal, often at the expense of the environment and working conditions.

Circular Fashion

A fashion model aimed at extending the lifespan of clothing by encouraging recycling, reuse, and repurposing of end-of-life textiles.

Sustainable Fashion

Practices for producing and consuming clothing that minimize environmental impact and respect workers' rights.

Recycled Polyester

A textile fiber made from recycled plastic, reducing the use of new materials and decreasing environmental impact.

Slow Fashion

A movement encouraging conscious consumption and the production of higher-quality clothing designed to last longer with reduced ecological impact.
