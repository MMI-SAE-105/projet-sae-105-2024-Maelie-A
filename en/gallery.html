Film Gallery

"Explore a selection of films that shed light on the challenges of sustainable fashion."

Written on October 20, 2024, at 5:00 PM

"Our rivers are dying. But we have the power to save them."

River Blue

Directed by David McIlvride and Roger Williams

This documentary explores how the denim industry and its chemical processes pollute rivers worldwide. It highlights the environmental damage caused by fashion and proposes solutions to create clothing without destroying aquatic ecosystems.

"Every garment we wear has a hidden cost. But who really pays the price?"

The True Cost

Directed by Andrew Morgan

This impactful film reveals the true human and environmental costs of fast fashion. It examines the working conditions of workers in developing countries and the industry's destructive impact on the environment while proposing solutions to change our consumption habits.

"Less is better. And it might be the key to saving our planet."

Minimalism: A Documentary About the Important Things

Directed by Matt D'Avella

This film, though broader than fashion, delves into the minimalist philosophy and how it can positively influence our consumption choices, including in fashion. It shows how reducing consumption can be a response to the excesses of fast fashion.

"Less is better. And it might be the key to saving our planet."
