About EcoDenim

"Learn more about EcoDenim."

Written on October 20, 2024, at 5:00 PM

EcoDenim is part of the "Prehistory of the Future" project, an initiative that invites us to imagine how our current objects will be perceived in a distant future, much like prehistoric artifacts are today.

This site explores the transformation of an iconic garment, jeans, by reinventing it for a more ecological and sustainable future.

Who Am I?

I am Maëlie Addario, a student in the MMI department at Montbéliard.

EcoDenim is a project I developed in 2024 as part of a pedagogical exercise titled "Prehistory of the Future."

My passion for sustainable fashion and innovation led me to explore how jeans, a timeless symbol, can be produced in a more environmentally friendly way.

Through this site, I aim to raise awareness among consumers about sustainable production practices and present concrete alternatives to reduce denim's ecological footprint.

This project reflects on the future of fashion and how we can redefine our consumption to protect our planet.

"Reinventing jeans for a greener future."
